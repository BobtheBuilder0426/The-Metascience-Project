# ReCAST: a causal, direction-aware method for muscle-rejuvenating metabolite cocktails

> **BOTTOM LINE**
> **ReCAST** is a discovery method that nominates a metabolite cocktail to rejuvenate aged skeletal muscle by a rule that inverts the inspiration work: supplement what *causally protects* aged muscle in the aging-reversing direction — not what rises during a youthful developmental transition. Its target is an aged→young signature of **uninjured** human muscle; nomination prioritizes **signed causal** evidence; the cocktail is assembled by **hallmark-complementarity**. Here I instantiate it as a proof-of-concept on real data: a computed 56-gene aging axis in GTEx muscle (n=716), tiered causal/mechanistic evidence for a hand-curated candidate set, and a hallmark set-cover. Top output: **Taurine + Nicotinamide Riboside**, chemically and mechanistically disjoint from the one-carbon cocktail. A data-contradicted candidate (spermidine) is rejected. I then specify a powered, blinded first pilot in aged mice whose **primary endpoint is ex-vivo specific force of uninjured muscle**, built to falsify the combination.

## 1. The problem and the governing idea

Metabolite cocktails for muscle are typically nominated from what changes during a youthful or regenerative state, then supplemented. Two failure modes follow, and ReCAST is built to avoid both.

**Direction (the governing idea).** "Add what goes up in youth" can be exactly backwards. Methionine and one-carbon flux rise during early cell-identity transitions, yet dietary methionine *restriction* is among the most reproducible pro-longevity interventions in mammals [fact: PMID 34518687 names reduced essential-amino-acid (incl. methionine) intake as a longevity lever]. A pipeline that supplements metabolites *because they rise* in a plastic state can therefore recommend the opposite of what extends healthspan. ReCAST instead advances a metabolite only when there is a **signed causal** argument that moving it in a specified direction protects aged muscle.

**Rejuvenation ≠ regeneration.** Satellite-cell activation, central-nucleated fibers, and Pax7⁺ counts are markers of injury repair. An agent that provokes low-grade damage-and-repair would light them up while reversing nothing about aging. ReCAST targets *uninjured* aged muscle, and the pilot's primary endpoint is muscle-intrinsic force with an explicit regeneration-confound detector.

## 2. The method, and what this instantiation actually computed

ReCAST runs in five stages (S1–S5) plus a benchmark. Below, each stage states the general specification and then, in italics, exactly what this first instantiation executed — so the method's ambition is not confused with the proof-of-concept evidence behind it.

**S1 — Target: the aged→young UNINJURED muscle signature.** *Specification:* a transcriptome-wide, cell-composition-deconvolved aging signature, replicated across independent cohorts, with injury/cytostasis negative controls. *This instantiation [computed]:* a **curated 56-gene panel** (nine mechanistic categories incl. a fibrosis/composition confound set) in GTEx skeletal muscle. I first confirmed the per-sample expression arrays align to the age/sex metadata: an expression-based sex call from XIST/RPS4Y1 matched the label for **543/543 samples called male** (a strict two-marker gate leaves the 260 labelled-female samples uncalled, so female precision is not asserted; no discordance was seen). I removed pathology-flagged and low-quality samples — **803 → 716 kept (87 excluded: 82 pathology-flagged for fibrosis/atrophy/inflammation/necrosis, of which 80 pathology-only + 2 also QC-failing, plus 5 QC-only for RIN<6)** — and fit each gene's partial correlation with age (GTEx decade brackets → midpoints, continuous), adjusting for sex, RIN, and ischemic time; BH-FDR.

*Figure 1. Aged skeletal-muscle molecular axis (curated 56-gene panel, uninjured GTEx muscle, n=716).*

The panel axis is biologically coherent (Figure 1) and, importantly, statements below are *within this curated panel*, not transcriptome-wide. The strongest age-increasing gene is *CDKN2A*/p16^INK4a (r=+0.29, 95% CI +0.22..+0.36, FDR=1×10⁻¹³) with *IL6*, *CDKN1A*/p21, *GLB1* also rising — canonical senescence/inflammaging, which validates the axis. Composition confounding is directly visible: *COL1A1/COL1A2/PDGFRA* rise with age (the reason for the pathology gate — though gating attenuates, it does **not** deconvolve within-cell from composition change). Among the panel's declining genes, the most consistent cluster is **redox/glutathione** (*GCLM*, *GSR*, *SOD2*; FDR≈8×10⁻⁴), with **NAD biosynthesis** (*NMNAT1*, FDR=0.019) and the mitochondrial-biogenesis regulator *ESRRA* (r=−0.21) also declining. Notably, polyamine-**synthesis** genes *DHPS/SMS/SRM* **increase** with age — evidence *against* a spermidine-restoration rationale.

**S2 — Signed causal prior (tiered).** *Specification:* candidate-specific signed genetics (MR / metabolite-QTL) per metabolite. *This instantiation [retrieved]:* a tiered prior — Tier 1, a direct causal *intervention* result (taurine); Tier 2, metabolome-wide MR against muscle-aging outcomes as a **direction/risk prior, not intervention proof** (outcome GWAS instruments exist: grip strength EFO_0006941, 850 associations; appendicular lean mass EFO_0004980, 1,740). Candidate-specific MR is largely **absent** for the non-taurine members — scored as penalized uncertainty (E2=1), not fabricated; no lifelong-exposure effect is translated into a finite-intervention effect. De-novo MR was **not** run here.

**S3 — Mechanism / signature reversal.** *Specification:* a quantitative perturbation-signature reversal score with specificity tests. *This instantiation [retrieved]:* a **qualitative literature score** of whether each agent's documented action reverses specific S1 hallmarks (senescence, redox, mitochondrial-quality, NAD).

**S4 — Combination by hallmark-complementarity.** *Specification:* a Reactome/KEGG/STRING-derived pathway network with a formal set-cover and redundancy metric. *This instantiation [computed on an expert-coded matrix]:* over the passing set, select the minimal cocktail covering the most distinct converging aging nodes at lowest redundancy, subject to a **distinctness filter** (members should not duplicate the one-carbon cocktail's chemistry). This selects *what to co-test* — it is explicitly **not** a synergy claim; interaction is *learned experimentally* in the pilot's Stage 1.

**S5 — Deliverability & safety.** *Specification:* ADMET, formulation, and aged-muscle exposure/target-engagement. *This instantiation [retrieved]:* documented **oral human-trial precedent** only; ADMET/exposure qualification is deferred to the pilot's Stage 1.

**Benchmark (consistency check, not a validation study).** With the scoring rubric applied, the nomination gate (panel target-fit ∧ documented hallmark reversal ∧ oral safety) recovers **taurine, urolithin A, and nicotinamide riboside** and **excludes spermidine**, which the panel data contradict. This is a consistency check on a small labelled set (three positives scored from their own literature; one negative); it is **not** a held-out blinded validation, and the planned differential-abundance and source-method baselines were not run. It shows the rubric is internally coherent and can reject a data-contradicted agent — no more.

**Top output: Taurine + Nicotinamide Riboside (NR).** The minimal, highest-evidence, mechanistically-orthogonal pair that is also chemically distinct from the one-carbon cocktail (Figure 2). Together they cover three of four aging nodes — senescence/inflammaging, mitochondrial-quality, and NAD-repletion. Redox/glutathione is a deliberate, stated residual gap. **Urolithin A** is the optional third (an orthogonal mitophagy route).

| Member | Causal direction [E2] | Mechanism / reversal [E3] | Oral delivery + safety [E4] |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Taurine** | Circulating taurine declines with age; restoration is causally protective (mice/monkeys) — *Science* 2023, **PMID 37289866** [fact] | Reduces cellular senescence, suppresses mitochondrial dysfunction, decreases DNA damage, attenuates inflammaging [fact: PMID 37289866]. These map onto the panel's age-up senescence axis (p16/IL6) [inference — the paper does not name these genes] | Completed anti-aging RCT **NCT05149716** (taurine vs placebo); oral [fact] |
| **Nicotinamide riboside** | NAD decline in aging is established; restore-NAD direction [inference]. No candidate-specific muscle-aging MR (E2=1) | Augments the NAD metabolome and induces an anti-inflammatory transcriptomic signature in **aged human skeletal muscle** — *Cell Rep* 2019, **PMID 31412242** [fact]. Same study reports it does so **without altering mitochondrial bioenergetics** — so NR is **not** credited a mitochondrial-quality point [fact] | Oral RCTs to Phase 4 **NCT05483465**; 1 g/day used in PMID 31412242 [fact] |
| *Urolithin A (optional)* | RCT-level, not genetic [inference] | Improved muscle strength (~12%) and mitochondrial biomarkers in **middle-aged** adults — *Cell Rep Med* 2022, **PMID 35584623** (NCT03464500). Caveat: the pre-registered **primary endpoint, peak power, was not met**; strength was secondary [fact] | Completed RCTs **NCT03464500 / NCT05735886 / NCT03283462** (Mitopure); oral [fact] |

**Why these and not the highest-scored redox agent.** GlyNAC (glycine + N-acetylcysteine) ranks second overall and is the best-supported redox agent — its RCTs directly improve glutathione deficiency, oxidative stress, and strength in older adults (*Clin Transl Med* 2021, PMID 33783984; *J Gerontol A* 2022, PMID 35975308), matching the panel's most consistent declining cluster. It is nonetheless **held out of the primary cocktail** because glycine and cysteine (as NAC) are components of the one-carbon cocktail, violating the distinctness requirement. This is a tension the method surfaces rather than hides: the cleanest panel target (redox) is best hit by a chemically non-distinct agent. GlyNAC is therefore the pre-specified **redox add-on** arm (Section 4).

**Distinct from the one-carbon cocktail.** No shared component; it acts through methyl-donor/one-carbon plasticity, whereas the ReCAST pick restores mitochondrial-quality, NAD, and anti-senescence tone in *homeostatic* aged muscle. Both members are age-**declining** metabolites (restore-direction) — the opposite selection logic to "add what rises in youth" [inference].

## 4. First pilot — falsify the combination in vivo

**Stage 1 (pre-animal, ≤8 weeks).** In aged-mouse primary myotubes: (a) confirm each constituent reaches and engages muscle (NAD-metabolome rise for NR; taurine uptake) — the deferred exposure/target-engagement gate; and (b) run a pre-specified **dose×time interaction test** against a declared Bliss-independence null with a withheld-condition prediction. Advance only if the combination beats the best single agent on aging-signature reversal plus respiration and is non-cytotoxic. This learns interaction *before* the animal test and confirms the specific-force variance used for powering.

**Stage 2 — focused efficacy.**
- **Estimand:** effect of the full cocktail versus vehicle on **ex-vivo specific force of an uninjured EDL** in aged mice at a fixed endpoint. **Experimental unit = individual animal; treatment assigned at the cage level** (shared water/diet), with cage modelled as a random effect.
- **Model:** C57BL/6JN (NIA aged colony), **both sexes**, **~22–24 months** at start; a young 4–6-month reference defines the young–old gap.
- **Intervention:** oral (drinking water or diet); doses/duration set from each agent's established efficacious regimen by allometric scaling, fixed at protocol registration (not asserted here).
- **Arms (staged to protect power):** *core* — vehicle, full cocktail, minus-taurine, minus-NR. *Context (secondary, hierarchically gated behind a positive primary)* — scramble (inert, calorie/osmolarity-matched), one-carbon-cocktail comparator, voluntary-wheel exercise, GlyNAC redox add-on.
- **Readouts (hierarchy pre-declared):** *primary* — ex-vivo specific force. *Supportive* — myofiber CSA/type, **central-nucleated-fiber count + fibrosis (the regeneration/remodeling confound detector)**, satellite-cell state, uninjured-quadriceps RNA-seq signature reversal, mitochondrial respiration, p16/p21. **Aging clock (DNAm/transcriptomic) — supportive, non-vetoing.**
- **Quantitative prediction & power [computed]:** the cocktail closes **≥50%** of the young–old specific-force gap (assume old 220, young 280 kN/m², so Δ≈+30 vs vehicle; SD≈30 → Cohen's d≈1.0). Two-sided t at α=0.05 needs **17/group** for 80% power; adjusting for cage clustering (design effect 1.15 at ICC 0.05, 4/cage) and 10% attrition gives **~22/group** (sex as a stratification covariate). The best single-agent drop-out closes **≤20%**; scramble ≈0.
- **Decision rule.** **Go** if cocktail > vehicle **and** > best drop-out **and** > scramble at the pre-set effect, with concordant molecular-signature reversal and **no** rise in central-nucleated fibers. **Falsified** if the cocktail is ≤ the best single agent, or a force gain co-travels with rising central-nucleated fibers (regeneration, not rejuvenation), or the aging clock moves without functional benefit.
- **Rigor:** pre-registered; variance-based power (above), re-run on Stage-1 SD before registration; cage-level randomization stratified on baseline grip and weight; blinded outcome assessment; Holm correction across the two drop-out comparisons; humane endpoints under IACUC oversight.

## 5. Assumptions, red-team, and scope limits
- **Regeneration masquerading as rejuvenation** → uninjured design; central-nucleated-fiber/fibrosis detector; force primary.
- **Wrong direction** → signed causal prior; "supplement what rises" traps rejected by construction (spermidine excluded on computed data).
- **MR overreach** → published MR used only as direction/risk prior; Stage-1 target-engagement required.
- **Synergy overclaim** → interaction learned against a declared null; drop-outs test necessity, not non-additivity.
- **Compositional / transient artifact** → confound gate (attenuates, does not deconvolve); persistence required.
- **Aging-clock artifact** → clock demoted to supportive.
- **Honest scope of this instantiation:** (1) S1 is a curated 56-gene panel, not a transcriptome-wide, deconvolved, replicated signature — "strongest/cleanest" claims are panel-internal, and gene-**expression** associations are not metabolite abundance or pathway flux; (2) the candidate set is a hand-curated shortlist of literature-supported agents, not an enumeration of a large metabolite space, so this ranks a shortlist rather than discovering across the full space; (3) the benchmark is a small-set consistency check, not a blinded held-out validation against baseline methods; (4) S2/S3/S4/S5 were executed at literature/expert-coded level, below their full quantitative specification. These are the parts to execute next to elevate the prototype to a full discovery run.
