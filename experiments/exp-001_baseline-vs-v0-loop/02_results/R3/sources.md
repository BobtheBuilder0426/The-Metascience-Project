## 2. Mechanistic pillars of the hypothesis (from the provided papers' bibliographies)

3. **Liu S, Fu S, … Cao Y, et al. (2021).** *Glycerol-3-phosphate biosynthesis regenerates cytosolic NAD⁺ to alleviate mitochondrial disease.* **Cell Metabolism 33, 1974–1987.** DOI: `10.1016/j.cmet.2021.06.013`. — Establishes the G3P shuttle as a cytosolic NAD⁺-regenerating pathway; the backbone of the redox-flux argument. *Verified: bibliography (Petrovic ref 51).*

4. **Módis K, Coletta C, Erdélyi K, Papapetropoulos A, Szabo C (2013).** *Intramitochondrial hydrogen sulfide production by 3-mercaptopyruvate sulfurtransferase maintains mitochondrial electron flow and supports cellular bioenergetics.* **FASEB J 27, 601–611.** — Low-level MPST-derived H₂S supports the ETC. *Verified: bibliography (Sprenger ref 46).*

5. **Filipović MR, Zivanovic J, Alvarez B, Banerjee R (2018).** *Chemical Biology of H₂S Signaling through Persulfidation.* **Chem Rev 118, 1253–1337.** DOI: `10.1021/acs.chemrev.7b00205`. — The persulfidation framework (how H₂S becomes a protein PTM). *Verified: bibliography (both papers).*

6. **Zivanovic J, Kouroussis E, Kohl JB, … Filipović MR (2019).** *Selective persulfide detection reveals evolutionarily conserved antiaging effects of S-sulfhydration.* **Cell Metabolism 30, 573–593.** — Persulfidation declines with age and its restoration improves stress resistance; motivates the persulfidation-activity link. *Verified: bibliography (Petrovic ref 26).*

7. **Lagoutte E, Mimoun S, … Bouillaud F (2010).** *Oxidation of hydrogen sulfide remains a priority in mammalian cells and causes reverse electron transfer in colonocytes.* **Biochim Biophys Acta 1797, 1500–1511.** — Sulfide:quinone oxidoreductase (SQR) routes H₂S-derived electrons into the CoQ pool; the mitochondrial arm of the hypothesis. *Verified: bibliography (Sprenger).*

8. **Szabo C, Ransy C, Módis K, … Bouillaud F (2014).** *Regulation of mitochondrial bioenergetic function by hydrogen sulfide. Part I.* **Br J Pharmacol 171, 2099–2122.** DOI: `10.1111/bph.12369`. — The biphasic (hormetic) dose–response of H₂S on respiration; grounds the dose caveat. *Verified: bibliography (Sprenger).*

9. **Ames BN (2018).** *Prolonging healthy aging: Longevity vitamins and proteins.* **PNAS 115, 10836–10844.** DOI: `10.1073/pnas.1809045115`. — Context for EGT as a "longevity vitamin." *Verified: bibliography (both papers).*

## 3. Prior art on EGT and exercise performance (novelty check)

10. **Fovet et al. (2022).** *Ergothioneine Improves Aerobic Performance Without Any Negative Effect on Early Muscle Recovery Signaling in Response to Acute Exercise.* **Frontiers in Physiology 13, 834597.** DOI: `10.3389/fphys.2022.834597` · PMC `PMC8864143`.
    - EGT (70 mg/kg/day, 1 wk) improved time-to-exhaustion by **+41.22% (p<0.01)** in **5-month-old C57BL/6J female mice** run at 70% of maximal aerobic speed, framed as a **secondary antioxidant that activates the NRF2 pathway**. This is the incumbent "antioxidant" explanation my pilot's enzyme-KO arm is designed to distinguish from the H₂S/persulfidation mechanism; its use of females also supports my both-sexes recommendation. *Verified: full text (fetched this session); title/DOI/PMC and the +41% effect confirmed against the article.*

11. **"Gene III Ergothioneine Ameliorates Exercise-Induced Fatigue by Attenuating Oxidative Stress, Inflammation, and Modulating the AMPK/PGC-1α Signaling Pathway" (bioRxiv, 2026).** DOI/URL: `10.64898/2026.02.19.706732` · `biorxiv.org/content/2026.02.19.706732v1` (the `10.64898` prefix is this environment's bioRxiv DOI convention; Unpaywall resolved it to the bioRxiv PDF).
    - Male C57BL/6 mice, 4-wk treadmill training + EGT (10/30/50 mg/kg): medium/high doses extended time-to-exhaustion and running distance and **reduced post-exercise lactic acid, LDH and BUN**, attributed to reduced oxidative stress/inflammation and **AMPK/PGC-1α–driven mitochondrial biogenesis**. Relevance: an independent study that reports the *same lactate drop* my hypothesis predicts, but attributes it to biogenesis rather than a lactate-threshold / cytosolic-redox shift — i.e. it does not test the threshold, so it neither confirms nor excludes my mechanism (the incumbent framing my pilot is designed to distinguish). *Verified: full text (fetched this session).*
