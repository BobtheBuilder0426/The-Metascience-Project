The answer

Prediction

EGT improves exercise performance by activating MPST in muscle mitochondria, raising H2S-derived persulfidation of ETC components.

Reasoning chain

  1. EGT accumulates in trained muscle mitochondria (Sprenger 2025).
  2. EGT binds + activates MPST → local H2S/persulfides.
  3. Persulfidation boosts Complex II throughput → more ATP under load.

Falsifiable prediction

In Mpst-KO mice, EGT loading will not improve treadmill time-to-exhaustion, whereas WT will gain ≥15%.

Group Predicted Δ endurance
WT + EGT +15–25%
Mpst-KO + EGT ~0%

Killer experiment

8-week EGT diet vs control in WT and Mpst-KO mice; treadmill VO2max + time-to-exhaustion; muscle persulfidation blot.
